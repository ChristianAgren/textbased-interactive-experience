You wake up. Something's wrong but you can't put your finger on what.
"...What happened..?"
You try and remember any details from earlier, but you can't even recall what day it is. You start feeling a piercing sensation in your head, like someone driving a nail through your head. For every passing attempt to remember a blow is struck to the nail, increasing the intensity and forcing you to stop and to start focusing elsewhere.

The room surrounding you is dark and damp. You're alone. In front of you is a chest and a note.

"Find the clues, find the antidote... or die"

The note seems almost like it's taunting you, childishly, but you can feel your heart sink to your stomach either way. Whoever wrote this note is serious.
